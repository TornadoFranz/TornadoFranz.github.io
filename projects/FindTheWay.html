FindTheWay

Unity / Mobile

About the Game:

FindTheWay is a casual mobile Game where the player has to remember in which order the hexagons appear on screen. The games gets harder the further the player gets.

About the Development:

The whole purpose of this project for me was to create a game i actually can release on the play store. Therefore i thought of the most minimalistic game i could possibly create to finally finish a project. To give the player atleast a bit of purpose i then implemented a highscore system so you could measure your self with others. There is realy not much to say about this one. It's a small, casual, minimalistic mobile game. Its not much but its mine.
